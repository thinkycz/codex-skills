# Freshness And Inference

Some questions need current verification. Others only need careful local inspection.

## Freshness Signals

Treat these as freshness-sensitive by default:

- APIs, models, pricing, versions, and SDK behavior
- schedules, regulations, company facts, and product availability
- bug reports tied to recent releases
- ecosystem recommendations that could shift quickly

## Inference Marking

When summarizing research, separate:

- confirmed
  directly supported by the evidence
- inferred
  a reasonable conclusion drawn from the evidence
- unresolved
  still missing proof or blocked by conflicting information

## Good Output Shape

- question
- evidence
- confirmed facts
- inference
- remaining uncertainty

Keep this short. The goal is to unblock the next decision, not to write a research memo.
