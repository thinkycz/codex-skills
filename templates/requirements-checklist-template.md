# Requirements Quality Checklist Template

Use this to review the written spec, not the implementation.

## Completeness

- Are all critical user journeys described?
- Are empty, loading, and failure states defined?
- Are role or permission differences explicit where relevant?

## Clarity

- Are ambiguous adjectives replaced with measurable language?
- Are acceptance signals testable?
- Are key terms used consistently?

## Consistency

- Do related requirements agree with each other?
- Do constraints and goals conflict anywhere?

## Coverage

- Are accessibility expectations explicit where relevant?
- Are integration failure modes addressed?
- Are important edge cases captured?
