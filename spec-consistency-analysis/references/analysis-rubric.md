# Analysis Rubric

Use this rubric when comparing spec, plan, and tasks.

## Contradiction

- the spec says one thing and the plan or tasks say another
- a required behavior is implemented differently in downstream docs

## Omission

- a significant requirement appears in the spec but has no plan or task coverage
- a planning phase has no mapped acceptance or verification consequence

## Drift

- the plan or tasks quietly expand or shrink scope without explicit decision logging
- acceptance expectations weaken across artifacts

## Ambiguity

- wording is too vague to support testable acceptance
- completion criteria are present but not measurable

## Reporting Standard

For each issue, note:

- the artifact surfaces involved
- why it matters
- the most appropriate next action
