# Stocktake Rubric

Use these decisions when reviewing the library.

## Keep

Choose `keep` when:

- the skill has a clear owner boundary
- the packaging is healthy
- the workflow is still useful and distinct

## Refresh

Choose `refresh` when:

- the skill is still worth keeping
- but metadata, references, prompts, or examples are stale

## Merge

Choose `merge` when:

- two skills regularly compete for the same prompts
- one owner plus a focused section or reference would be clearer

## Split

Choose `split` when:

- one skill mixes two workflows with different triggers or acceptance bars
- the core file is becoming a blended mega-skill

## Retire

Choose `retire` when:

- the skill is redundant
- the owner boundary is no longer meaningful
- the content is weak enough that repo-first discovery or another skill already covers it
