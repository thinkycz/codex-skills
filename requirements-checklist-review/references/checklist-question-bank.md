# Checklist Question Bank

Use these when a spec needs stronger requirement-quality prompts.

## Completeness

- What are the primary user journeys?
- Which states must exist for loading, empty, success, and failure?
- What is intentionally out of scope?

## Clarity

- What does done look like in observable terms?
- Which behaviors are mandatory versus optional?
- Which numbers, thresholds, or limits matter?

## Consistency

- Do naming and rules stay consistent across sections?
- Do constraints conflict with goals anywhere?

## Coverage

- What edge cases could break the expected flow?
- What accessibility requirements matter here?
- How should integration or dependency failure behave?
