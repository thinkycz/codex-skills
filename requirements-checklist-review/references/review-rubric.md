# Review Rubric

Use this rubric when reviewing a written spec before planning.

## Completeness

- Are the critical user journeys described?
- Are important states present: loading, empty, error, success?
- Are key dependencies, constraints, or integrations explicit?

## Clarity

- Can the requirement be implemented without guessing?
- Are vague adjectives replaced with measurable language?
- Are completion signals testable?

## Consistency

- Do requirements conflict with each other?
- Are terms and entities named consistently?
- Do non-goals and goals agree?

## Coverage

- Are accessibility-sensitive expectations explicit where relevant?
- Are major edge cases covered?
- Are integration failure modes or fallback rules addressed?
