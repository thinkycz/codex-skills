# Delegation Ladder

Keep work local when:
- the next lead step depends directly on the result
- the task is still ambiguous
- the task defines architecture or shared contracts
- the task is likely to reveal the real shape of the broader work

Delegate to a worker when:
- the goal is clear
- the write scope is bounded
- the slice is lower-risk than the integration path
- review and reintegration are straightforward

Escalate back to the lead path when:
- the worker repeatedly struggles
- the task starts changing shared contracts or architecture
- new ambiguity appears that changes dependency order
